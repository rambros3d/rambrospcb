Hi, I make PCB design tutorials for my students in **Tamil**.

I teach PCB design from the basics-> advanced, and even teach them to get it fabricated.

My tutorials are available on YouTube and this website, and it will **always be free**.

> **I'm not a teacher, I'm a PCB designer**. I dont sell courses.


---

# Collaboration Policy

I accept these types of collaborations.

### 1) PCB Manufacturers

I always tell my students to order the boards which they design. So if you're a PCB manufacturer, I can feature your service if you **provide a considerable discount for the students**.
Example: JLCPCB - check [this video](https://youtu.be/4SAFcLJZraI?t=221)

### 2) Product & services
A product or service which is relevant to engineering can be accepted (example: PCB, CAD, 3D printing, CNC machining, etc)

If a product is sent to me for testing, I will choose to feature it only if it is of good quality and useful to the students.

### 3) Factory Tour

If you want to flex your production capabilities, You can also give me a tour of your factory. Me and my team will visit, interview you and explain the whole production process to my students.

---

## Non-negotiable
- Product promotions will never be placed inside tutorials.
- Promotional content will be published separately.

#### To avoid conflict of interest, **I do not accept payments in exchange for promotions**. No exceptions.
